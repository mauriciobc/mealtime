# Weight Goals

Set weight goals to track your cat's health.

---

## Creating a Goal

1. Go to **Weight** at `/weight`
2. Click **"New Goal"** or **"Set Goal"**
3. Configure:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Target Weight | Weight you want to achieve |
| Deadline | Deadline to reach the goal |
| Milestones | Intermediate steps (optional) |

4. Click **"Save"**

---

## Tracking Progress

After setting a goal:
- View the **progress** towards the goal
- See intermediate **milestones**
- Track the **trend** on the chart

---

## Editing Goals

1. On the weight page, find the goals section
2. Click **"Edit"** on the existing goal
3. Update the settings
4. Save the changes

---

## Removing Goals

To remove a goal:
1. Go to the goals section
2. Click **"Delete"** or **"Remove"**
3. Confirm the action
