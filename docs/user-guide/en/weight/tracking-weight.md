# Weight Tracking

Monitor your cats' weight to ensure their health and well-being.

---

## Recording Weight

1. Go to **Weight** at `/weight`
2. Click **"Record Weight"**
3. Fill in the information:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Weight | Weight in kg |
| Date | Date of weighing |
| Notes | Optional notes |

4. Click **"Save"**

---

## Viewing History

On the weight page you will find:
- **Current Weight**: Last recorded weight
- **Trend Chart**: Weight evolution (30, 60 or 90 days)
- **Recent History**: List of recent records

---

## Selecting Cats

If you have multiple cats:
- Use the dropdown menu to select which cat to view
- Each cat has its own history and chart

---

## View Periods

Choose the chart period:
- **30 Days**: Short-term view
- **60 Days**: Medium-term view
- **90 Days**: Long-term view

---

## Tips

- Weigh the cat regularly (weekly or monthly)
- Use the same scale for greater accuracy
- Record after feeding or fasting (be consistent)
