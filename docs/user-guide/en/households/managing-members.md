# Managing Members

Learn how to invite and manage members in your household.

---

## Inviting Members

1. Go to **Households** at `/households`
2. Select the desired household
3. Click **"Invite"** or **"Manage"**
4. Enter the email of the person you want to invite
5. Click **"Send Invite"**

The invitation will be sent via email. The invitee will need to accept to join the household.

---

## Accepting Invitations

When you receive an invitation:
1. You will receive a notification via email
2. Access the invitation link or go to `/join`
3. Enter the invitation code
4. Accept the terms and confirm

---

## Removing Members

Only administrators can remove members:
1. Go to the household details
2. Find the member in the list
3. Click **"Remove"**

---

## Invitation Codes

Each household has a unique invitation code that can be:
- Shared with family members
- Used to join the household
- Regenerated at any time (invalidates the previous one)
