# Notifications

Configure and manage MealTime notifications to never forget a feeding.

---

## Types of Notifications

MealTime offers two types of notifications:

1. **Push Notifications**: Notifications in the browser/app
2. **In-App Notifications**: Alerts within the application

---

## Configuring Notifications

1. Go to **Settings** at `/settings`
2. Click **"Edit notifications"**
3. Configure your preferences:
   - Enable/disable push notifications
   - Enable/disable email notifications
   - Choose which types of notifications to receive

---

## Schedule Notifications

When a schedule is active, you will receive:
- A push notification at the configured time
- Reminder to feed the cat
- Option to record the feeding directly

---

## Notification Center

Access your notifications:
- Click the bell icon in the top bar
- View unread notifications
- Mark as read or delete

---

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Not receiving notifications | Check browser permissions |
| Notifications disabled | Enable in app settings |
| Too many alerts | Adjust schedule frequency |
