# Profile and Settings

Customize your MealTime experience by managing your profile and preferences.

---

## Accessing Settings

1. Click the user icon in the upper right corner
2. Select **"Settings"** or go to `/settings`

---

## Your Profile

In the **Information** tab you will find:
- Display name
- Registered email
- Registration date
- Username

To edit: click **"Edit profile"**

---

## Appearance

Configure the app's appearance:

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Theme | Light, Dark or System |
| Accent Color | Customize the app color |

---

## Regional Preferences

Configure:
- **Language**: Portuguese (Brazil)
- **Timezone**: Your local timezone

This affects:
- Date formatting
- Notification times

---

## Managing Household

In settings you can:
- View the current household
- Access member management
- Household settings

---

## Logout

To log out of the account:
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Click **"Logout"**
3. Confirm the action

---

## Tabs Guide

On the profile page (`/profile`), there are tabs:

| Tab | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| Information | Your profile data |
| Homes | Households you participate in |
| My Cats | Cats you've added |
