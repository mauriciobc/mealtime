# Statistics

View charts and detailed data about your cats' feeding.

---

## Accessing Statistics

1. Go to **Statistics** at `/statistics`
2. View the charts and data
3. Use filters by period and cat

---

## Available Data

On the statistics page you will find:

- **Total Feedings**: Number of recorded meals
- **Average Portion**: Average amount per meal
- **Trends Chart**: Evolution over time
- **By Cat**: Individual statistics per cat

---

## Interpreting Data

| Metric | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Total Feedings | Meals recorded in the period |
| Average Portion | Average amount per meal |
| Trend | Whether the cat is eating more or less |

---

## No Data

If the page shows "No Data":
- More feedings need to be recorded
- Data appears after some records
