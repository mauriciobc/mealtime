## Quick Record

The fastest way to record a feeding:

1. On the **home screen (Dashboard)**, locate the floating **"+"** button in the bottom right corner
2. Tap the **"+"** button or **"Registrar nova alimentação"** (Record new feeding)
3. A modal opens automatically
4. **Select the cat** (required)
5. Adjust the **amount** in grams if necessary
6. Tap **"Salvar"** (Save) ✅

Done! The feeding was recorded in less than 10 seconds.

---

## Complete Record

For a detailed record with more information:

1. Go to **Feedings** at `/feedings`
2. Click the **"Registrar"** (Register) button at the top of the page
3. Fill in the fields:

| Field | Type | Description | Required |
|-------|------|-------------|----------|
| Cat | Selection | Select the cat | ✅ Yes |
| Date/Time | DateTime | Time of meal (default: now) | ✅ Yes |
| Portion | Number | Amount in grams | ✅ Yes |
| Meal Type | Selection | Type of meal | ❌ No |
| Food Type | Selection | Type of food | ❌ No |
| Notes | Text | Observations | ❌ No |

4. Add notes if necessary
5. Click **"Salvar"** (Save)

## Feeding History

To view the complete history:

1. Go to **Feedings** at `/feedings`
2. See the complete list of records ordered by date
3. Each card shows: cat, amount, time

---

## Searching Records

1. On the history page (`/feedings`), use the search field
2. Search by: cat name, notes, date
3. Results appear in real-time

---

## Sorting

- **Most recent first** (default): Most recent at the top
- **Oldest first**: Oldest at the top

---

## Tips

- **Record right after feeding** for greater time accuracy
- **Use the floating button** for quick daily records
- **Add notes** about appetite or behaviors (e.g., "ate little")
- **Set default portions** in the cat's profile for auto-fill
- **Review history** weekly to identify patterns
