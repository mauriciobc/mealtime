# Feeding History

View and manage all recorded feedings of your cats.

---

## Accessing History

1. Go to **Feedings** at `/feedings`
2. See the complete list of records
3. Use filters to search for specific records

---

## History Features

On the history page you will find:

- **Complete List**: All feeding records
- **Search**: Look for cat, notes or user
- **Sorting**: Most recent or oldest
- **Details**: Portion, meal type, food type

---

## Sorting and Filters

- **Sort**: Most recent first or oldest
- **Search**: Filter by cat name, notes or user
- **By period**: View by day, week or month
